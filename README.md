# GreenGrid 🌱

GreenGrid is a peer-to-peer renewable energy exchange platform built during a 36-hour Green Hackathon.

The platform connects renewable energy producers, consumers, and investors in one decentralized ecosystem, making clean energy trading more accessible, transparent, and community-driven.

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Many renewable energy producers generate surplus energy that often goes unused or is difficult to distribute efficiently.

Traditional energy systems rely heavily on centralized distribution, creating inefficiencies, higher costs, and reduced accessibility for local communities.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

GreenGrid enables users to:

- Trade renewable energy directly
- Discover local clean energy sources
- Monitor live transactions
- Connect producers, consumers, and investors
- Promote sustainable and decentralized energy systems

---

# ✨ Features

## ⚡ Energy Marketplace
Browse renewable energy listings from solar, wind, and biogas producers.

## 📊 Live Transaction Feed
Track real-time energy trades happening across the network.

## 🌍 Renewable Energy Ecosystem
Separate onboarding for:
- Producers
- Consumers
- Investors

## 📱 Responsive Modern UI
Futuristic and responsive interface designed for accessibility and usability.

## 🌱 Sustainability Focused
Encourages clean energy adoption and community-driven energy exchange.
